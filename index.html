Maus Review

Feb 27, 2024

Review

Maus is a graphic novel about a holocaust survivor written by his son. He turns people into animals to make the story more immersive. It uncovers how people had to escape the holocaust and the reactions to what the Nazi's were doing. I enjoyed the book because of how easy it was to read and how the artwork really makes the story easier to connect with and understand. I would reccomened reading Maus if you have not read it before.

Dune Review

Feb 28, 2024

Review

Dune is a Sci-Fi movie about a gifted young man named Paul. His job is to travel to planets to make sure his family and friends can surive for generations. Fighting rages over the planet and he has to overcome his doubt. The movie had great production and was directed by Denis Villeneuve. I would recomened watching the movie if you ever get the opportunity.
